Our Story

Founded in the heart of Andhra Pradesh's mineral-rich landscape, Red Rock Minerals Pvt Ltd has grown from a regional mining operation into a trusted name in laterite mineral supply. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we serve industries across India and beyond.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted laterite mining company, setting benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to responsibly extract and deliver premium-grade laterite minerals while creating value for our stakeholders, communities, and the environment.
