SECTORS

Powering Diverse Industries

Our high-quality laterite minerals and ores serve as critical raw materials across a wide range of industrial sectors, from heavy manufacturing to agriculture.

Steel & Iron

Our high-grade laterite ore with Fe content of 45-55% is ideal for steel manufacturing and iron smelting operations. Used as a direct feed in blast furnaces.

Cement Manufacturing

Laterite serves as a key raw material in cement production, providing essential iron and alumina content for clinker manufacturing.

Construction & Roads

Laterite aggregates and lumps are widely used in road construction, building foundations, and as a durable construction material across infrastructure projects.

Refractory Industry

High-alumina laterite and bauxite from our mines are used to manufacture heat-resistant refractory bricks and linings for furnaces and kilns.

Chemical Industry

Laterite fines serve as raw material for chemical processing, including the extraction of iron and aluminum compounds for industrial applications.

Agriculture

Laterite-based soil amendments help improve soil structure and drainage. Our finely processed minerals support agricultural land improvement.
